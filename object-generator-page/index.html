object generator
what is this?
i made this in 2021, one of my first p5 sketches. the intention was to make something like digital cloud watching. i often find myself seeing vases or lamps.

another thing, modern image generation often relies on the following:
  • very large datasets (often biased and taken without consent)
  • resource consuming algorithms

    • this generator has no dataset. it's just a random scribbles with symmetry added in. there are lots of sustainable and positive ways we can generate!
